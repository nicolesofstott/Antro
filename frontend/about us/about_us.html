About Antro

Our manifesto

Art doesn’t exist in isolation—neither should artists. We created this space for those who dare to create, question, and push culture forward. A space where emerging artists and cultural practitioners can sell their work, find opportunities, and sustain their practice without navigating endless barriers. Opportunities shouldn’t be scattered across the internet, hidden behind paywalls, or locked within exclusive circles. That’s why we bring them all together—open calls, residencies, collaborations—all in one place, accessible to all. We believe in a future where artists don’t just survive, but thrive. This is our step towards connectivity. Join us.
